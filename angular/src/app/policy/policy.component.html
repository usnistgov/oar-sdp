This NIST Science Data Portal and Public Data Repository operate in support of the NIST Plan for Providing Access to Federally Funded Research in accordance with government policy and law. The plan is documented through a series of Directives, referenced on the Public Access to NIST Research webpage.
NIST maintains an organization Enterprise Data Inventory (EDI) and generates a public data catalog complying with Federal Enterprise Data Resources guidance. The NIST Public Data Repository includes this catalog as part of its core metadata. A standard machine-readable format, the DCAT-US (Project Open Data) metadata schema representation of this catalog is available here. Additionally, this catalog is harvested and aggregated in the government Data.gov website as part of the Department of Commerce agency data collection.

The Science Data Portal and Public Data Repository collectively serve NIST Open Data policy and exemplify capabilities supporting new public law, Foundations for Evidence-Based Policymaking Act (115-435) Title II, Open Government Data Act (2019).

As part of NIST strategic efforts to develop infrastructure supporting public access and discoverability to government research datasets, this page will be updated to reflect changes in content and services as they pertain to policy and law.
