In accordance with U.S. Government Open Data Policy and the NIST Plan for providing public access to the results of federally funded research data, NIST maintains a publicly accessible listing of available data, the NIST Public Dataset List (json).

The government open data policy for managing information as an asset may be found on the Project Open Data website.

Please refer to the NIST Open Data Strategy for Implementation and Processes for additional organizational detailed description.
