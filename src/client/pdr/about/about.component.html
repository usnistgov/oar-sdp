In accordance with U.S. Government Open Data Policy and the NIST Plan for providing public access to the results of federally funded research data, the NIST Science Data Portal provides a discovery and exploration tool for publically available datasets at NIST.

Data sources include Standard Reference Data, Data publications and free standing data made available through the NIST Public Dataset List (json) enterprise data inventory, and key legacy datasets.

Public datasets are assigned Digital Object Identifier (DOI) to increase the discovery and access to research output. These DOIs are registered in DataCite and provide globally unique persistent identifiers.

This portal is designed and developed with data.gov Project Open Data standards and principles. The portal software is hosted in the usnistgov github repository.
